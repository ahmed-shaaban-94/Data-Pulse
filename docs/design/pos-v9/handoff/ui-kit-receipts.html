A · PAPER

Not flat — textured

The thermal sheet has subtle horizontal scan lines, torn zigzag top & bottom, and a warm off-white tone (#f5f2ec). Shadows ground it on the dark canvas.

B · HIERARCHY

One serif moment

Fraunces shows up only on the grand total and the insurance split & ETA. Everything else stays monospace — the way thermal prints naturally.

C · CLINICAL

Black counsel block

Counseling & rider notes get inverted ink (black fill, paper text). This is the one moment the pharmacist's voice should interrupt the transaction.

D · BILINGUAL

AR leads, EN follows

Arabic is primary; English is the small italic line underneath. Invoice numbers, timestamps, and codes stay Latin for scanner compatibility.
